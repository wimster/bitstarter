My Stop Coach is a cross-platform social app.

Our mission is building a strong community aiding in stopping self-destructive addictive behavior and other bad habits.

It's not easy for people with an addiction to reach out for help. My Stop Coach wil down this barrier and make it easy to connect with your peers with similar habits and in similar situations.

My Stop Coach provides you with 24/7 sponsors worldwide, while protecting your privacy and offering a save social network.
